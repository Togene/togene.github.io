About

My name is Eugene Martens, currently a student at QUT, studying a Bachelors of IT - Majoring in Computer Science.

Software Development is my main focus, with an approach to full stack development. I'm fascinated with problem solving and enjoy delivering results for the most complex solutions. I also employ creative ways to build code out. From my studies, I've enjoyed collaborating with my peers and working on projects as a team. I think one of the most positive aspects of Software Development is the continuous learning aspect and in my spare time I build projects that utilise new frameworks and technologies.

I've previously studied a Bachelors of Game Design and appreciate building games, starting from scratch. So much thought, creativity and passion goes into the development of a game, which in turn means always consistently learning something new, a large part of why I enjoy development. Visual aesthetics and design of a game are my main points of interest, as art, conceptional design and graphics programming are areas I am most familiar and adept at.
